alphabet 'circular'

In my second semester, I developed an alphabet called "Circular" based only on elementary geometric forms (such as circle, triangle and square). This alphabet, named Circular, was then presented in two different forms.
First, I created a poster to present my alphabet in a pleasing and attention-drawing way. It shows off the unique character and gives the impression, the letters float over the page. The colors help the alphabet to appear friendly and attractive.
The second poster I created was an informational poster. Here, the whole alphabet as well as the name are to be seen. Also, there is an informative text which explains the rules I created the alphabet by. This poster is held in black and white to underline its informative character and to improve readability. At the end of the semester, I also developed a short image clip for this alphabet. It was also my final project for the course "Typography and Layout".
