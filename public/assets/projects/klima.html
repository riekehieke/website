logo 'klimainitiative'

A logo for the departement of climate change at the university of applied sciences bremerhaven. The logo can be interpreted as two hands holding the earth but also as an eye or waves. This demonstrates the main focus of climate change activists: protect the earth and keep an eye on everything. The waves, as well as the colors, reference the location of the university: directly at the shore of the north sea.
All in all, it is a sleek, modern yet recognizable logo with a cleraly visible connection to the north sea and the subject climate change.

- december 2017
