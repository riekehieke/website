prototype medical chatbot 'lara'

prototype of a medical chatbot called lara. It helps the user to analyse symptoms, make doctor appointments and set reminders to take medication. The concept also includes the possibility to renew prescriptions for selected drugs directly in the app and avoid long wating hours at the doctors office. it also shows the nearest pharmacies.
The prototype was designed with the focus on good ux/ui design and realised with the help of the onsen ui template and html, css & javascript.
