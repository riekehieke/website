portfolio website "digital media production"

in the autumn of 2017, me and my colleagues Max M. Schneider and jonas kuske started to design and develop a portfolio website for our bachelor-course "digital media production" at the university of applied sciences in bremerhaven, germany. the website showcases work from students from different years and is supposed to help people, that are interested in the course to get a better impression of what the students learn in that course.
the website is built on the javascript-framework vue.js to acheive a flawless single-page-experience. the structure and styling are done but the implementation of acutal content still needs to be done.
