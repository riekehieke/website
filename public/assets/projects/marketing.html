marketing-project 'typro'

Marketing concept for a fictional app. The fictional app can scan any billboard, poster or text and tell the user which font was used. The marketing concept consists of a landing page various posters and an image clip. I created an app mockup, a billboard and roll-ups in a clean and minimalistic deisgn. This design mirrors the main design aspects that were also used for the landing page and speaks mainly to design enthusiasts and designers.
