marketing-project 'typro'

For my university-course "marketing", me and my fellow students had to create an imaginary product to set up a marketing report and create advertisements for this product. My team chose to "create" an app called "typro". Its main function is to scan fonts you see in your daily life with the use of the smartphones camera. The app is made for professional designers that are always looking for new typefaces to use in projects. Because of this target group, I decided to keep my designs simple and minimalistic. The logo was created by my fellow student Max M. Schneider and I created the banners, the billboard and also an app mock-up.
