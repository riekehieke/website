corporate design 'landessportbund bremen'

Imaginary Corporate Design for the Landessportbund Bremen. The Landessportbund Bremen is the biggest sport-organization in Bremen with aproximatly 160,000 members. The logo consists of a modern geometric form with a cut-out area and sleek typography. The variable colour palette serves to underline the modern and fresh character of the logo.
The logo is part of a modular system. It can be enhanced by a second module to display certain branches of this big organization.
All the corporate design aspects are bundled in the corporate design manual. To take a look inside, click here (german only).
